G3000 H Event List

Below is a list of H events used by the base G3000. Note that all H events are case-sensitive.

GDU softkey events​

AS3000_PFD_1_SOFTKEYS_[index]​

PFD 1 softkey press. [index] is the index of the softkey (1 through 12 from left to right).

AS3000_PFD_2_SOFTKEYS_[index]​

PFD 2 softkey press. [index] is the index of the softkey (1 through 12 from left to right).

Horizontal GTC events​

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_TopKnob_Large_INC​

Horizontal GTC (580) outer concentric (top) knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_TopKnob_Large_DEC​

Horizontal GTC (580) outer concentric (top) knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_TopKnob_Small_INC​

Horizontal GTC (580) inner concentric (top) knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_TopKnob_Small_DEC​

Horizontal GTC (580) inner concentric (top) knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_TopKnob_Push​

Horizontal GTC (580) inner concentric (top) knob short push. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_TopKnob_Push_Long​

Horizontal GTC (580) inner concentric (top) knob long push. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_SoftKey_1​

Horizontal GTC (580) upper softkey press. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_SoftKey_2​

Horizontal GTC (580) middle softkey press. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_SoftKey_3​

Horizontal GTC (580) lower softkey press. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_BottomKnob_Small_INC​

Horizontal GTC (580) lower knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_BottomKnob_Small_DEC​

Horizontal GTC (580) lower knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Horizontal_[index]_BottomKnob_Push​

Horizontal GTC (580) lower knob push. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

Vertical GTC events​

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_Joystick_INC​

Vertical GTC (57x) joystick increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_Joystick_DEC​

Vertical GTC (57x) joystick decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_Joystick_Up​

Vertical GTC (57x) joystick upward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_Joystick_Down​

Vertical GTC (57x) joystick downward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_Joystick_Left​

Vertical GTC (57x) joystick leftward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_Joystick_Right​

Vertical GTC (57x) joystick rightward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_Joystick_Push​

Vertical GTC (57x) joystick push. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_MiddleKnob_INC​

Vertical GTC (57x) middle knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_MiddleKnob_DEC​

Vertical GTC (57x) middle knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_MiddleKnob_Push​

Vertical GTC (57x) middle knob push. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_RightKnob_Large_INC​

Vertical GTC (57x) outer concentric (right) knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_RightKnob_Large_DEC​

Vertical GTC (57x) outer concentric (right) knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_RightKnob_Small_INC​

Vertical GTC (57x) inner concentric (right) knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_RightKnob_Small_DEC​

Vertical GTC (57x) inner concentric (right) knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_RightKnob_Push​

Vertical GTC (57x) inner concentric (right) knob short push. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

AS3000_TSC_Vertical_[index]_RightKnob_Push_Long​

Vertical GTC (57x) inner concentric (right) knob long push. [index] is the instrument index of the GTC (1, 2, 3, etc).

PFD Controller events​

AS3000_PFD_[index]_RANGE_INC​

PFD controller joystick increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_RANGE_DEC​

PFD controller joystick decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_JOYSTICK_UP​

PFD controller joystick upward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_JOYSTICK_DOWN​

PFD controller joystick downward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_JOYSTICK_LEFT​

PFD controller joystick leftward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_JOYSTICK_RIGHT​

PFD controller joystick rightward deflection. [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_JOYSTICK_PUSH​

PFD controller joystick push. [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

Autopilot events​

Garmin_AP_HDG_Sync​

Autopilot selected HDG sync.

Miscellaneous events​

AS3000_PFD_[index]_BARO_INC​

PFD baro knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_BARO_DEC​

PFD baro knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_BARO_PUSH​

PFD baro knob push. [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_CRS_INC​

PFD course knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_CRS_DEC​

PFD course knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).

AS3000_PFD_[index]_CRS_PUSH​

PFD course knob push. [index] is the instrument index of the PFD (1 or 2).
