G3X Touch H Event List

Below is a list of H events used by the base G3X Touch. Note that all H events are case-sensitive.

GDU button events​

AS3X_Touch_[index]_NRST_Push​

GDU NRST button press. [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_DirectTo_Push​

GDU Direct-To button press. [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Menu_Push​

GDU Menu button press. [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Back_Push​

GDU Back button press. [index] is the index of the GDU.

GDU 460 knob events​

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Outer_L_INC​

GDU Left outer knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Outer_L_DEC​

GDU Left outer knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_L_INC​

GDU Left inner knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_L_DEC​

GDU Left inner knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_L_PUSH​

GDU Left inner knob press. [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_L_PUSH_LONG​

GDU Left inner knob long press. [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Outer_R_INC​

GDU Right outer knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Outer_R_DEC​

GDU Right outer knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_R_INC​

GDU Right inner knob increment (clockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_R_DEC​

GDU Right inner knob decrement (counterclockwise rotation). [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_R_PUSH​

GDU Right inner knob press. [index] is the index of the GDU.

AS3X_Touch_[index]_Knob_Inner_R_PUSH_LONG​

GDU Right inner knob long press. [index] is the index of the GDU.
